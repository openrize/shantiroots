Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2025

ShantiRoots ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at shantiroots.vercel.app or make a purchase from us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and shipping/billing address when you place an order

  • Messages and information when you contact us via our contact form

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our site, including IP address, browser type, referring URLs, and pages viewed (via cookies and analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of our site may not function properly.

5. Data Security

We use SSL encryption across our site and industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for any accounts.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email

To exercise these rights, contact us at openrize@gmail.com.

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
